Savings Account

A savings account is a great ways for students to save money. When looking for a savings account, you must look for anccount with higher AYP and lower service fee. If you are paying a higher service fee you could be losing more money than you save. Many of the accounts offer free savings account and have competitives rates, so choose wisely.

Checkings Account

A checking account is great when it comes to accessibility, it offers debit cards and checks which are mainly for spending. Money in checkings account are more accessible, they are mainly used to pay bills, make purchases, and withdraw money from ATM. Typically they earn little or no interest, best way to choose a checkings account is to go with the one with no service fee that way you do not lose money from you account.

Investment Account

An investemnt account, allows you to buy stocks, bonds, mutual funds and other assets with money you deposit in your account. You can open an account with either an investment company or firm and usually there is no fee to open this type of account. Once your account is open and funded, you can actually buy investments using the money you have deposited. Investment accounts can be a great way to save money for future, especially for students; the earlier you start the better off you are, with this comes a great sense of responsibility and can polish your management skills.
